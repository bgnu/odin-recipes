Egg rice

Here is the incredibly delicious EGG RICE.

How can you make this lovely dish? Here. Just below this text. Have a look - and have a cook! Just on the next line.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Rice
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Buy eggs
  2. Cook the egg
  3. Open some precooked rice
  4. Dump the egg and the rice into a bowl
  5. Chop an onion into quarters
  6. Add the onion to the bowl
  7. Done. Enjoy
